Ethos

An imaginitive, jack of all programming trades with a focus in web technologies, game design and UI. Through the act of play, I aim to achieve lasting interactions between human and machine to both aid in learning and making experiences enjoyable and familiar

My Identity

I'm a young, full stack engineer ready to learn all the web has to offer me. My last position was a Junior Virtual Reality Developer at Virtro Entertainment working with Unity, the LAMP stackLinux|Apache|MySQL|PHP and Mixed Reality Technologies. In this position I worked with a small team porting games to Virtual Reality, developing web apps and debugging/designing games within Virtual Reality. In my university career, I'm learning designfocusing on Human Computer Interaction and User Interface Design, low level computingUnix|C|Memory_Management, game designUnity|Git|Maya|C#|User_Experience, web design and developmentHTML|CSS|Javascript|Typography|Color, relational database management, and networkingin progress

In my spare time I like to play video games and experiment with different forms of art. I value comfort and essential simplicity above all else, feeling most relaxed in quiet and peaceful environments

Being of both Italian and Chinese decent, culture and inclusion is a big part of my life. I try to embrace and integrate all the multicultural traditions that Canada offers us. I really believe that tradition is a connecting medium that needs to be embraced because of the plethora of knowledge and inspiration that is just waiting to be incorporated in everyday life. I intend to experience as much culture as I can and open myself to new ideas and inspirations as I go through life
